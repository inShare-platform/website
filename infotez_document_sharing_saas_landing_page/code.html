Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got answers. If you don't see what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

How does Infotez track website and document engagement?

expand_more

Infotez uses a proprietary, cookieless tracking script for websites and generates unique, trackable links for documents. This allows us to gather engagement data like views, time spent on each page, and download activity without relying on third-party cookies, ensuring privacy compliance.

What kind of AI features do you offer?

expand_more

Our AI analyzes engagement patterns to provide a predictive lead score, highlighting your most interested prospects. It also identifies key areas of interest within your documents and can suggest optimal follow-up actions to help you convert leads more effectively.

Is my data secure with Infotez?

expand_more

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Our platform also offers robust security features like password protection, access controls, and link expiration to ensure your shared documents remain confidential and secure.

Can I customize the pricing plans?

expand_more

Yes! While our Lite and Pro plans cover most use cases, we offer a Custom Plan for enterprises and businesses with specific needs. You can choose a custom number of URLs, select specific features, and get dedicated support. Contact our sales team to build your perfect plan.

What file types do you support for document sharing?

expand_more

Infotez supports a wide range of file types, including PDF, DOCX, PPTX, XLSX, and more. Our platform is designed to handle the most common business document formats, so you can upload and share with confidence.
